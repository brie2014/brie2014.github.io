Chicken Salad

  • 1 can of chicken breast
  • 2 T mayonnaise
  • 1/2 c of grapes, quartered
  • 2 stalks of chopped celery
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  1. Shred chicken in a bowl.
  2. Stir in mayonnaise, grapes, celery, salt, and pepper.
  3. Eat plain or serve on croissants or rolls.

Recipe from: Brienna Sambrano

This is great with crackers or bread or can simply be eaten plain. Add apples or pickles for extra crunch.
