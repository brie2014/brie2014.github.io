Gingersnap Cookies

  • 2 1/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup packed brown sugar
  • 3/4 cup shortening or cooking oil
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1 tsp ground ginger
  • 1 tsp ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 tsp ground cloves
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  1. In a mixing bowl, combine about HALF of the flour, the brown sugar, shortening, molasses, egg, baking soda, ginger, cinnamon, and cloves.
  2. Beat with an electric mixer on medium to high speed till thoroughly combined. Beat in remaining flour.
  3. Shape dough into 1-inch balls and roll in sugar. Place 2 inches apart on an ungreased cookie sheet.
  4. Bake in a 375Â° oven for 8-10 minutes or till set and tops are crackled. Cool cookies on a wire rack. Makes about 48 cookies.

Recipe from: Better Homes and Gardens cookbook

After they cool, make sure to store in an airtight container to maintain their softness.
