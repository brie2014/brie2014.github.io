Italian Sausage Sheet Pan

  • 2 large carrots
  • 2 red potatoes
  • 1 medium zucchini
  • 2 bell peppers (any color)
  • 16 oz smoked turkey polish sausage
  • 2 T Italian seasoning
  • 1/2 T garlic powder
  • 1/8 tsp red pepper flakes
  • 1/2 tsp onion powder
  • 4 1/2 T olive oil
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • 1/3 cup shredded parmeasan cheese (optional)
  1. Preheat oven to 400Â°F.
  2. Line a large baking sheet with parchment paper or aluminum foil.
  3. Peel and thinly slice the carrots.
  4. Chop the red potatoes into small pieces.
  5. Halve the zucchini, then cut into thick slices.
  6. Clean and chop bell pepper into medium-size pieces.
  7. Slice the sausage into coins, then chop the coins into halves.
  8. Combine the veggies and sausage in a large bowl.
  9. Drizzle the olive oil over the veggie/sausage mixture, then toss in the spices. Mix until everything is well coated.
  10. Pour mixture onto prepared baking sheet and bake for 15 minutes. Remove pan from oven, stir, and bake an additional 10-20 minutes.
  11. Remove fom Oven, sprinkle parmesan on top (optional) and serve plain or with rice or quinoa.

Recipe from: Tiffany Stewart

You can subsitute other veggies such as broccoli into here, or add more or less of your favorite veggies!
