Target Audience

This site is for people in the region of Southeast Idaho, specifically around the towns of Preston, Soda Springs, and Fish Haven. It targets those who want to stay updated on weather and related local news in one place. Users will likely be residents of the specfied region that have access to a connected device, who are comfortable doing basic web-browsing. Most of these will be 16-60. Users may visit the site using a variety of devices.

Scenarios

  • What's the weather going to be like in my local area today?
  • What's the weather going to be like in my local area in the next two weeks?
  • Do I need extra time to get to work today?
  • Are there any current events going on in my town?
  • I want to know details about important weather events in my area.
