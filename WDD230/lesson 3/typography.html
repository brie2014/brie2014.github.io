Typography

Header Font

 All text in the header section will be in this easy to read, catchy font.

Heading Font

 All headings not in the header throughout the site will be in this font and color.
Regular Paragraph Text Most text on the site will be this font, size, and color. This is for any text against a light background.
Colored Paragraph Occasional text may be on a light-colored background. It will maintain the size and color of regular paragraphs.
Light Paragraph Text Any text against a dark background will be in this color. This includes text in the header.
