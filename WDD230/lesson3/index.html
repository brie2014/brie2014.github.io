Purpose

The purpose of this site is to provide weather and other local updates to people in the southeast Idaho towns of Preston, Soda Springs, and Fish Haven. The home page will have a current weather story with images. The site will feature sections for each town that include the weather conditions and other information for the local town's people. A map will also be included for user convenience. Users of this site should be able to easily find weather and other updates by using this site.
