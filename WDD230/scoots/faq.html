Is there an age limit for the scooters and vehicles?

We would love to help your family explore Cozumel. No one under 12 years of age are allowed to ride on the scooters. However, our ATVs and Jeep Wranglers are excellent options for anyone traveling with kids.

Does the renter pay for fuel?

All of our vehicles come with a full tank of fuel. The renters are responsbile for fueling up the vehicle as needed. When you return your vehicle, you may choose to return it with a full tank of fuel or pay a $25 fuel charge.

How many miles am I allowed to drive?

All of Scoots vehicles come with unlimited mileage for your convenience!

How soon in advance should I make a reservation?

You must make your reservations at least 24 hours in advance to qualify for the special rate. Other than that, the sooner your reserve, the better selection of rentals we will have available.

What do I need to bring when I pickup my rental?

A valid drivers license is required in hand at time of rental. If you did not pre-pay, a method of payment will also be required.

How much is the security deposit?

A $50 security deposit is required to confirm reservations.

What payment methods do you accept?

We currently accept Google Pay, cash, credit card, debit card, or PayPal.

Do I need to have insurance to rent from Scoots?

Liability insurance is included for free with every rental.

Where do I pickup my rental?

Rentals may be picked up at either location. If you have a 2+ day rental, Scoots can also arrange for free delivery to any Cozumel hotel, Airbnb, or private residence.

Does it matter which location I return my rental to?

Rentals may be returned to either location. If you have a 2+ day rental, Scoots can also arrange for free pickup from any Cozumel hotel, Airbnb, or private residence.
